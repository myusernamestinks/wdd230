Site Purpose

The Katy Chamber of Commerce website serves two primary purposes:

  • Provides detailed information to those that visit this site with information on local business and available services.
  • offeres support for local business and activites sponsored by the community. Allows residents and visitors oppurtunies to have fun within our community.

Scenarios

  1. What local events will be held this month for residents and visitors?
  2. What are available resources for those with questions regarding planning and hold events
  3. What are foreseeable events happening within our community?
